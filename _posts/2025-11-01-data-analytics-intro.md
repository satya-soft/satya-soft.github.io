Data analytics is the process of examining data sets to draw conclusions about the information they contain. For businesses, this means turning raw data into actionable insights.

## Descriptive Analytics

Understanding what has happened in the past is the first step. Descriptive analytics helps businesses review performance and identify historical trends.

## Predictive Analytics

Predictive analytics uses statistical models to forecast future outcomes. This helps businesses anticipate market changes and customer behavior.

## Prescriptive Analytics

The final stage is prescriptive analytics, which suggests actions to benefit from the predictions. It answers the question, "What should we do?"
