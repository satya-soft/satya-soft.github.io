Cybersecurity is an ongoing battle. With threats becoming more sophisticated, organizations must stay vigilant.

## Zero Trust Architecture

Adopting a Zero Trust model ensures that no user or device is trusted by default. Verification is required for every access request.

## Regular Training

Human error remains a significant vulnerability. Regular security training for employees can help prevent phishing attacks and other social engineering exploits.

## Incident Response Plan

Having a well-defined incident response plan ensures that your organization can react quickly and effectively in the event of a breach.
