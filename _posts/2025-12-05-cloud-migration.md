Cloud migration is a critical step for modern businesses. However, it requires careful planning and execution to avoid disruptions.

## Assess Your Portfolio

Before migrating, it's essential to assess your current application portfolio. Determine which applications are ready for the cloud and which require refactoring.

## Choose the Right Provider

Whether it's AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud, choosing the right provider depends on your specific business needs and technical requirements.

## Security First

Security should be a top priority during migration. Implement robust security measures to protect your data in transit and at rest.
