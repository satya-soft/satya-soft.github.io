## About the Role

We are looking for a Data Scientist to join our growing analytics team. You will be analyzing large datasets to extract meaningful insights and build machine learning models.

## Responsibilities

*   Analyze complex datasets to identify trends and patterns.
*   Build and deploy machine learning models.
*   Visualize data to communicate findings to stakeholders.
*   Collaborate with engineers to integrate models into production.

## Requirements

*   Master's or PhD in Computer Science, Statistics, or related field.
*   Proficiency in Python and libraries like Pandas, Scikit-learn, TensorFlow.
*   Experience with SQL and data warehousing.
*   Strong communication skills.
